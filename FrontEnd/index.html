About Us

Telect is a web-based tutoring platform on a mission to give every child access to tutoring resources no matter their financial circumstances. We do this using a Name Your Price strategy where both parents and tutors set what they can pay and earn for a session.This price can even be zero, so those who otherwise couldn't afford help can still receive it. Through subsidizing a portion of our revenue and volunteer tutors we will be able to achieve this cost for middle and low income families.
